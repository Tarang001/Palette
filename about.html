Who Are We
Our Logo

A palette is the essential tool of an artist, representing the very essence of creativity and artistic expression. By adopting the palette as the club logo, the art club symbolizes its commitment to nurturing and celebrating the creative talents within its members.

It brings together painters, sketchers, sculptors, and artists of various disciplines under one emblem, fostering a sense of unity and inclusivity within the club.

Our Approach

Inviting various people from various art background to conduct a workshop delivering knowledge about various arts.

collaborating with art clubs and societies of nearby institutes (recently with iitd soon with du)

Conducting bimonthly sessions where club members can give there ideas and suggestions.

Running a self dependent club where the club members can teach skills to fellow members .
